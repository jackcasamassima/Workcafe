# Workcafe 

## Jack Casamassima

## The app I plan to build will provide users with data to inform them of the "workability" of cafes or third spaces around them. 
### The intended users are on-the-go individuals who are in search of places to study, work, or relax that will cater to their needs. 

## Planned Features:
### An interactive map of the user's area which allows them to discover cafes around them and see the details of their "Workcafe Score"
### Scores for cafe elements such as outlets, quietness, busyness, comfort, and vibe
### The ability to add reviews and favorite visited cafes
